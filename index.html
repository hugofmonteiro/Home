Microbiome Engineering

A career dedicated to exploring how microbes, host, and environments interact - through the lens of animal physiology, microbiology, bioinformatics, and artificial intelligence

Research Contact

Hugo F. Monteiro

I am passionate about reprogramming microbial ecosystems to solve real-world challenges in livestock sustainability. I do not just ask scientific questions — I build frameworks to answer them, combining microbiology, fermentation, bioinformatics, and AI to understand and engineer complex biological systems. My work is fueled by grit, curiosity, and a belief that meaningful change happens when science meets purpose. Stick around and follow the journey.
